Update your CANable

Updater

  • First, move your "Boot" jumper to the boot position, or hold down the "Boot" button on your CANable Pro, then plug it in
  • Next, select the firmware you want to flash and press "Connect and Update" below
  • Nothing showing up in the Chrome connection window on Windows? Run this utility to fix your drivers.
  • Nothing showing up on Linux? You likely need to add a udev rule or run Chrome as root

CANable builds are compiled direcly from the candlelight and slcan repositories on github
